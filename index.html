Intro

Welcome to Cowboy Cocktails! My name is Isiah and I hope you are ready to saddle up for a good time! I specialize in Bartending/Mixology and would love to to be your bartender for the night. I do special events, private events, business parties, holiday parties or anything that involves letting the hair down and having a hoot n hallerin' good time! As the name mentions, my bartending uniform consists of a cowboy hat, cowboy boots, suspenders and nice pair of levis or dress pants. If that is not your style or preference, that is completely fine and I will be more than happy to wear something else or change the style for the night.

Parties and Events are always a good time but can also be stressful. I will make sure that your special night or special event is stress free so you don’t need to worry about making drinks, setting up, cleaning up or worrying about your guests being taken care of. I have been bartending for 6 years, in several different states and pride myself on great customer service.

Please reach out if you have any questions or if you would like to book an amazing night of drinking and partying with me and these dancing boots ;) My instagram page is @cowboycocktail and my email is cowboycocktail3@gmail.com. I can’t wait to meet you, saddle up for the night and let the hair down together!!
